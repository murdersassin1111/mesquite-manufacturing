FAQ

Common Questions
from Engineers

Can't find your answer? Submit an RFQ and we'll respond within 24 hours.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We hold tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across our CNC machining and milling operations. Tighter requirements can be discussed during your quote.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds rotational movement to the standard 3-axis setup, enabling intricate multi-face geometries in a single setup. This means higher complexity and higher volume at lower cost than multi-setups.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites across both CNC machining and 3D printing services. Specific alloys and grades can be discussed in your quote request.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes. We offer rapid prototype development for design validation and scale to full production runs — often for the same part as it moves through your development cycle.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — precision CNC milling is a core service. Our milling capabilities include multi-axis work for complex geometries on metals, plastics, and composites.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing services cover traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — with tolerances matching our CNC work at ±0.001″–0.005″.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form below with your project details. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week — including weekends. The quote is completely free with no obligation.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7 and can accommodate urgent timelines. Specific lead times depend on your part complexity, quantity, and material — contact us for a project-specific estimate.

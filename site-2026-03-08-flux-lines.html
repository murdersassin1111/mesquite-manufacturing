FAQ

Common Questions

Everything engineers and procurement teams need to know before submitting an RFQ.

QWhat CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We routinely achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances across our CNC machining, milling, and 3D printing services. Tight-tolerance parts are our specialty.

QWhat is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds rotational movement (A-axis) to standard 3-axis machining. This enables intricate geometries and complex cuts in a single setup — at high volume and lower cost than competitors.

QWhat materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites — covering the full spectrum of modern engineering materials for both prototyping and production runs.

QCan you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes. Prototype Development and Precision Machining production runs are both core services. We support your full product lifecycle from first concept to volume manufacturing.

QDo you offer CNC milling?

Absolutely. Precision CNC milling is one of our primary services — backed by 4th axis capability for complex geometries and tight tolerances down to ±0.001″.

QDo you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service delivers ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances across traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — the same precision standards as our CNC work.

QHow do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — share your part description, material, and timeline. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. No obligation, completely free.

QHow fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7 and work around your timeline. Lead time depends on part complexity, quantity, and material. Contact us with your project details for a specific lead time estimate.

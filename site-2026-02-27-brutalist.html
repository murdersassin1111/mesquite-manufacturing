FAQ

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We routinely hold ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances on both CNC machining and 3D printing. Tight-tolerance, high-precision work is our specialty.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth axis CNC adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and undercuts at high volume — and at lower cost than competitors who rely on multiple setups.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites. If you're unsure of the best material for your application, our team will advise you during the quoting process.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes. We offer rapid prototype development for design validation as well as full production machining. Many clients start with a prototype and scale directly into production with us.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Precision CNC milling is a core service. Our milling capabilities span 3-axis and 4th axis work, delivering complex geometries with tight tolerance control.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes — additive manufacturing to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals. 3D printing is ideal for complex parts, fast prototypes, and low-volume runs.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page with your project details. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. No obligation, completely free.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7 and move fast. Lead time depends on part complexity, material, and volume. Contact us through the RFQ form for a specific timeline on your project.

Common Questions

GOT QUESTIONS?
WE'VE GOT ANSWERS

Everything engineers and buyers need to know before requesting a quote.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We hold tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across our CNC machining, milling, and 3D printing operations — covering even the most demanding precision part requirements.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds rotational movement to standard 3-axis machining. This enables intricate geometries, multi-face features, and undercuts in a single setup — producing complex parts at high volume with lower cost than competitors using multiple setups.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites — including traditional plastics, innovative composite materials, and metal alloys. Contact us with your material spec and we'll confirm compatibility.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes. We offer dedicated Prototype Development for rapid concept validation, and our 4th axis CNC is purpose-built for high-volume production runs at competitive cost.

Do you offer CNC milling specifically?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core capability. Our CNC milling achieves the same ±0.001″–±0.005″ tolerances with 4th axis options available for complex part geometries.

Do you offer 3D printing services?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service delivers to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in plastics, composites, and metals — bridging the gap between prototyping speed and production precision.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — include your part description, material, quantity, tolerance, and timeline. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. Quotes are free with no obligation.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7 and work around the clock to supply the best service possible. Lead time depends on your project scope — submit your RFQ and we'll provide a timeline specific to your requirements.

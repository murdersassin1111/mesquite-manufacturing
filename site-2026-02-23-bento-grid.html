Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before submitting your RFQ for CNC machining or milling.

What tolerances can you achieve?

±0.001″–0.005″ depending on material and geometry — across metals, plastics, and composites.

What is 4th axis CNC machining?

Adds a rotational axis enabling complex geometries at high volume and lower cost than standard 3-axis setups.

What materials do you work with?

Metals, engineering plastics, and advanced composites — fully supported across all four of our service lines.

Prototypes AND production runs?

Yes — both. Prototype development for validation, then 4th axis CNC for high-volume production runs.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — CNC milling is a core capability within our precision machining services, using 4th axis technology.

How do I get a quote?

Fill the RFQ form below. Describe your part, material, quantity, and tolerance needs. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes — additive manufacturing to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances using plastics, composites, and metals.

How fast is your turnaround?

We work 24/7. Submit your RFQ with your timeline and we'll confirm lead time specific to your project.

Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Engineering-level answers to the questions we hear most often.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We machine to ±0.001″–0.005″ across CNC machining, milling, and 3D printing services — consistently maintained through our quality control process.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds rotational movement to 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries in a single setup at high production volumes. This significantly reduces cost versus multi-setup alternatives.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites across all services. Contact us with your specific material for a compatibility confirmation and recommendation.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes. Prototype development and production-scale CNC machining are both core services. We handle small validation runs and high-volume production — often for the same customer on the same part.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — precision CNC milling is a core service. Our 4th axis mills enable highly complex milled geometries at competitive pricing versus traditional 3-axis setups.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service produces parts to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, quantity, tolerance, material, and timeline. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7 and work continuously to supply the best service possible. Lead time depends on your specific project — contact us and we'll give you an accurate timeline immediately.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve? +
We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across our CNC machining, CNC milling, and 3D printing services. Our 4th axis CNC technology and precision measurement tools ensure every part meets spec before it ships.
What is fourth axis (4th axis) CNC machining? +
Fourth axis CNC machining adds rotational movement (typically the A-axis) to the standard XYZ axes. This enables machining of intricate geometries around a part's circumference without re-fixturing — allowing us to manufacture complex, high-volume parts at significantly lower cost than conventional 3-axis shops.
What materials do you machine? +
We work with metals (aluminum, steel, stainless steel, titanium, brass, copper), plastics (ABS, Delrin, UHMW, nylon, PEEK, polycarbonate), and composites (carbon fiber, fiberglass, G-10). Not sure if your material qualifies? Submit an RFQ and we'll confirm.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs? +
Yes — both. Our Prototype Development service is built for rapid design validation and early-stage iteration. Our Precision CNC Machining service scales to high-volume production with 4th axis technology that keeps unit costs competitive at scale. Many of our clients start with prototypes and graduate to production with us.
Do you offer CNC milling? +
Yes. CNC milling is a core service at Mesquite Manufacturing. We perform precision CNC milling with 4th axis capability, achieving ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances across metals, plastics, and composites. Our milling services handle everything from simple pockets to complex multi-surface profiles.
Do you offer 3D printing? +
Yes. Our additive manufacturing service prints in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals to tolerances of ±0.001″–0.005″. 3D printing is particularly powerful for complex internal geometries, lattice structures, and designs that conventional machining cannot access efficiently.
How do I get a quote? +
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — it takes about 2 minutes. Include your part description, material, quantity, tolerance requirements, and timeline. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. You can also attach a drawing or CAD file for the most accurate quote.
How fast can you turn around a project? +
We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead times vary by project complexity, quantity, and material availability. Submit your RFQ and we'll provide a specific timeline based on your requirements — we're always looking for ways to accelerate delivery without compromising precision.

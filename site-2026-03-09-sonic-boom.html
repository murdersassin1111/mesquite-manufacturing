FAQ

Engineer's Questions
Answered

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on both CNC machining and 3D printing operations — covering the full range from precision prototype to high-volume production.
What is fourth axis CNC machining?
Fourth (4th) Axis CNC adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis machining — enabling intricate geometries, complex contours, and high-volume part production at significantly lower cost than multi-setup conventional machining.
What materials do you machine?
We machine metals, plastics, and composites across all services. Contact us with your specific material requirements and we'll confirm compatibility and process selection.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
Yes — we offer both prototype development (rapid, iterative, cost-effective) and full production machining with 4th Axis CNC for high-volume parts. We support the entire product lifecycle.
Do you offer CNC milling?
Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service. Our 4th Axis capability extends standard milling to complex geometries and undercuts that would otherwise require multiple setups.
Do you offer 3D printing?
Yes — additive manufacturing with ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals. We apply the same precision standards to 3D printing as to CNC machining.
How do I get a quote?
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your project, upload a drawing if you have one, and hit submit. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week.
How fast can you turn around a project?
We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Turnaround depends on project complexity and quantity — contact us with your specific timeline and we'll give you a direct answer.

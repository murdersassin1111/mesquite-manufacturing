Technical FAQ

Questions from Engineers Like You

Everything you need to know before submitting your RFQ.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We routinely machine to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances across metals, plastics, and composites. Tight-tolerance requirements are our specialty — contact us with your specific drawing for a tolerance capability review.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth axis (4th axis) CNC adds a rotary axis to standard 3-axis milling, enabling intricate geometries, helical features, and compound angles in a single setup — at high volume and lower cost than multi-setup alternatives.

What materials do you machine?

We machine a wide range of metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, titanium, brass), engineering plastics (PEEK, Delrin, nylon, UHMW), and composites. Have an unusual material? Ask us — we likely have experience with it.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — our Prototype Development service covers rapid iteration and design validation, while our CNC machining capability handles full production volume. Many customers start with prototypes and scale to production with us.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service. We operate advanced multi-axis milling centers for 3-axis and 4th axis work, handling everything from simple profiles to complex contoured surfaces.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes — our additive manufacturing service produces functional parts in plastics, composites, and metals to the same ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances. Ideal for complex geometries, low-volume parts, and rapid prototypes.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — it takes less than 2 minutes. Describe your part, upload a drawing or CAD file if available, and we'll respond with a custom quote within 24 hours, 7 days a week. No obligation.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7 and prioritize fast turnaround. Lead time depends on part complexity, quantity, and current workload. Contact us with your specific project — we'll give you an accurate timeline on your RFQ.

FAQ

Questions Engineers Actually Ask

Straight answers to the technical and process questions we hear from engineers and buyers every day.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve? +
We routinely achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances across CNC machining, CNC milling, and 3D printing operations. Tighter tolerances are achievable for specific geometries — describe your requirements in your RFQ and we'll confirm capability upfront.
What is fourth axis CNC machining? +
4th axis CNC adds a rotational axis (A-axis) to standard 3-axis machining, enabling parts to be machined on multiple faces in a single setup. This unlocks intricate geometries, reduces setup time, and allows us to produce high-volume complex parts at significantly lower cost than traditional methods.
What materials do you machine? +
We machine a full range of metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, brass, titanium, and more), engineering plastics (PEEK, Delrin, ABS, nylon, etc.), and composite materials. Not sure if your material qualifies? Include it in your RFQ and we'll confirm.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs? +
Absolutely. Our prototype development service handles single parts and small iterations for design validation, while our production machining capabilities support high-volume runs. Many customers start with a prototype and scale directly to production with us.
Do you offer CNC milling? +
Yes — precision CNC milling is one of our core services. We offer 3-axis and 4th axis CNC milling for flat profiles, contoured surfaces, slots, pockets, and complex multi-face geometries. CNC milling is available for prototypes and production volumes.
Do you offer 3D printing? +
Yes. Our additive manufacturing service achieves ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances and supports traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals. 3D printing is ideal for complex geometries, rapid prototyping, and low-volume production.
How do I get a quote? +
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, select your service and material, and attach a drawing or CAD file if available. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week with a custom quote tailored to your project.
How fast can you turn around a project? +
We operate 24/7 to supply the best service possible, and lead times vary based on part complexity, quantity, and material availability. Submit your RFQ and we'll provide a specific timeline as part of your custom quote — we're known for working with tight schedules.

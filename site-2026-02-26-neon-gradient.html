// FAQ

Questions Engineers Ask

Everything you need to know before submitting your RFQ.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve? ▾

We regularly achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across CNC machining and 3D printing operations. Discuss your specific tolerance requirements in your RFQ for a tailored assessment.

What is fourth axis CNC machining? ▾

Fourth (4th) Axis CNC adds rotational movement around the X-axis, enabling our machines to create intricate geometries that a standard 3-axis setup cannot achieve. This allows us to manufacture complex, high-volume parts at lower cost than competitors using traditional methods.

What materials do you machine? ▾

We work with a broad range of metals, plastics, and composites. Whether you need aluminum, steel, titanium, engineering plastics, or advanced composites — tell us your material in the RFQ and we'll confirm capability.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs? ▾

Yes. We offer full Prototype Development for rapid design validation as well as Precision Machining for high-volume production. Our 4th Axis CNC technology is specifically suited to scaling from prototype to production without re-tooling.

Do you offer CNC milling? ▾

Yes — precision CNC milling is a core service at Mesquite Manufacturing. We perform milling operations across a wide range of materials and part geometries, with tolerances to ±0.001″.

Do you offer 3D printing? ▾

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service achieves tolerances of ±0.001″–0.005″ using traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metal materials. Ideal for complex geometries and rapid prototyping.

How do I get a quote? ▾

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, select your service and material, and optionally attach a drawing or CAD file. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. It's free and carries no obligation.

How fast can you turn around a project? ▾

We're available 24/7 and work to accommodate your timeline. Lead time depends on the complexity, quantity, and material of your specific project. Submit your RFQ with your deadline and we'll give you an honest timeline.

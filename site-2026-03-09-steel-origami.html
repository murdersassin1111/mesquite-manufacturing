FAQ

Engineer Questions,
Direct Answers

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ depending on geometry, material, and feature complexity — meeting the demands of precision engineering applications across aerospace, defense, industrial, and consumer sectors.

What is Fourth Axis (4th Axis) CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds a rotational axis (A-axis) to standard 3-axis machining. This enables complex, intricate geometries — helical features, contoured surfaces, off-angle bores — at high volume and lower cost than multi-setup alternatives.

What materials do you machine?

We machine metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, brass, titanium and more), engineering plastics (Delrin, PEEK, nylon, polycarbonate), and composites. Our 3D printing covers plastics, innovative composites, and metal materials.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — prototype development and production volume are both core services. Many customers start with rapid prototyping, validate their design, and transition directly to production runs with us.

Do you offer CNC milling specifically?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service. We run multi-axis milling operations capable of complex 3D surfaces, pockets, slots, contours, and threaded features with ±0.001″ precision.

Do you offer 3D printing / additive manufacturing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing services cover plastics, composites, and metals to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances — making 3D printing viable for functional, load-bearing, and precision components.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — include part description, material, quantity, tolerance, and attach any drawings or CAD files. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. Free quote, no obligation.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We operate 24/7 and work to supply the best service possible on your timeline. Lead time depends on part complexity, quantity, and material availability — contact us with your project details for a specific lead time estimate.

Technical Reference

Frequently Asked Questions

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We routinely achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on our CNC machining and milling operations. For 3D printing, we achieve the same ±0.001″–0.005″ range across materials.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth axis (4th axis) CNC adds rotational movement to the standard X, Y, Z axes. This enables intricate geometries and complex part profiles at high volume — at lower cost than traditional 3-axis + multiple setups.

What materials do you machine?

We work with a wide range of materials including metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, titanium, etc.), engineering plastics, and composites. Contact us to confirm your specific material.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — we offer dedicated Prototype Development for early-stage validation and full CNC Machining for production volumes. Many customers run prototypes with us before scaling to production.

Do you offer CNC milling specifically?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service. We handle surface milling, profile milling, and complex 4th-axis milling operations for a wide range of part types.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes — our additive manufacturing service covers traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals, with tolerances of ±0.001″–0.005″. Great for prototypes and production parts alike.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, attach a drawing or CAD file if you have one, and we'll respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week, with a tailored quote.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead times depend on project complexity, material, and volume — submit an RFQ with your timeline and we'll give you a specific answer.

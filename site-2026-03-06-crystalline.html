FAQ

Common Questions from Engineers

Everything you need to know before submitting your RFQ.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We machine to ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances depending on geometry, material, and process. Our 4th axis CNC handles the tightest specs on complex parts.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds rotational movement to the standard 3-axis setup, enabling intricate geometries and undercuts at high volume — at lower cost than traditional multi-setup machining.

What materials do you machine?

We work in metals, plastics, and composites. Contact us with your specific material — we handle a wide range from aluminum and steel to engineering plastics and advanced composites.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — prototype development and high-volume production are both core services. We scale with your project from first concept to full production.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service. Our 4th axis milling centers produce highly accurate parts with complex contours and tight tolerances.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes — additive manufacturing to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals for rapid prototyping and production.

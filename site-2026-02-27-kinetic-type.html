Frequently Asked

CNC Machining
FAQ

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We routinely achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across our CNC machining and milling operations. Our 4th Axis CNC equipment enables tighter geometries at scale.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth axis CNC machining adds a rotational axis (A-axis) to three-axis milling — enabling intricate designs, undercuts, and complex geometries in a single setup. This enables high-volume production at lower cost than competitors.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites. Whether you need aluminum, steel, stainless, PEEK, or composite laminates — tell us your spec and we'll confirm material availability.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes. We offer dedicated Prototype Development for concept validation, and full Precision CNC Machining for production volumes. Same precision, same team, at any quantity.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Absolutely. Precision CNC Milling is a core service at Mesquite Manufacturing. Our milling capabilities cover 3-axis and 4th-axis operations for flat, contoured, and complex-profile parts.

Do you offer 3D printing services?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service achieves tolerances of ±0.001″–0.005″ in plastics, composites, and metals — ideal for prototypes or complex geometries that are difficult to machine.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, material, quantity, and timeline. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week, with a custom quote tailored to your project.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We are available 24/7 and work to supply the best service possible. Lead time depends on complexity and quantity — contact us with your project details for an accurate timeline.

Frequently Asked

Everything You Need to Know

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We routinely achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on both CNC machined and 3D printed parts — covering everything from tight-fit precision components to general-purpose production parts.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth (4th) axis CNC adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and complex contours without repositioning. This lets us manufacture high-volume precision parts at lower cost than competitors.

What materials do you machine?

We work with a wide range of materials including metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, brass), plastics (PEEK, Delrin, nylon, polycarbonate), and composites — both in CNC machining and 3D printing.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Absolutely. We offer rapid prototype development for concept validation and iterative refinement, and scale seamlessly into high-volume production runs — often on the same equipment.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — CNC milling is a core service. We use advanced multi-axis CNC milling centers to produce precision flat surfaces, contours, pockets, and complex 3D profiles to exact tolerances.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service produces parts in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — achieving tolerances of ±0.001″–0.005″, making it viable for functional and production parts.

How do I get a quote?

Simply fill out our RFQ form below. Describe your part, attach your drawing or CAD file if you have one, and we'll respond with a tailored quote within 24 hours, 7 days a week.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We operate 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead times depend on part complexity and volume — contact us with your project details and we'll give you a specific timeline.

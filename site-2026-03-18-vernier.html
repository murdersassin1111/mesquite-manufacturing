Frequently Asked Questions

Engineer's Guide to
Working With Us

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We machine to ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ depending on part geometry and material. Our 4th axis CNC systems enable high-precision, complex geometries at these tolerances.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds rotational movement to the standard X, Y, Z axes — enabling intricate geometries, undercuts, and complex profiles in a single setup. This translates to lower cost at high volume.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites across all four of our services. Not sure which material is right for your part? Tell us your application and we'll recommend the best fit.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — prototype development and production machining are both core services. Many customers start with a prototype run and scale directly into production with us.

Do you offer CNC milling specifically?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is one of our core capabilities — high accuracy, tight tolerances, complex surface profiles, and multi-axis work all available.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service holds ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances and works with traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form below — include your part description, material, quantity, and tolerance requirements. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7 and work to supply the best service possible on your timeline. Contact us with your specific project details and we'll confirm lead time.

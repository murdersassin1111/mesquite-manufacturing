FAQ

Common Questions from Engineers

Everything you need to know before submitting your RFQ — straight answers, no fluff.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
+
We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across both CNC machining and 3D printing operations. Tighter tolerances for critical features are achievable — describe your requirements in the RFQ form and we'll advise.
What is fourth axis CNC machining?
+
Fourth-axis (4th axis) CNC machining adds rotational movement to the standard 3-axis setup. This enables intricate geometries and complex features in a single setup — eliminating repositioning errors and dramatically reducing cost for high-volume runs. Our 4th axis capability is a key cost advantage over 3-axis competitors.
What materials do you machine?
+
We work with metals, plastics, and composites. This includes aluminum, steel, stainless steel, titanium, engineering plastics (PEEK, Delrin, Nylon), and advanced composite materials. List your material in the RFQ and we'll confirm feasibility.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
+
Yes. Both prototype development (rapid, iterative, small quantity) and production machining (high-volume, cost-optimized) are core services. Our 4th-axis CNC is specifically suited to high-volume production at lower per-part cost.
Do you offer CNC milling?
+
Yes — precision CNC milling is a core capability. Our milling operations cover 3-axis and 4th-axis work, enabling complex contoured surfaces, pockets, slots, and features that standard turning cannot achieve.
Do you offer 3D printing?
+
Yes. Our additive manufacturing service delivers tolerances of ±0.001″–0.005″ across traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals. 3D printing is ideal for complex geometries, rapid prototyping, and production of parts that are difficult or costly to machine.
How do I get a quote?
+
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, material, tolerance, quantity, and timeline. Attach a drawing or CAD file if available. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week, with a tailored quote and process recommendation.
How fast can you turn around a project?
+
We're available 24/7 and work continuously to supply the best service possible. Lead time depends on your project complexity, material, quantity, and current capacity. Submit your RFQ and include your required timeline — we'll give you a concrete answer.

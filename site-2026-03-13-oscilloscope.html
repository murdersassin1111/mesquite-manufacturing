Frequently Asked Questions

Everything engineers need to know before submitting an RFQ.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
We achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances on CNC machining and 3D printing, depending on material and geometry. Our 4th Axis CNC equipment is calibrated for precision on every run.
What is fourth axis CNC machining?
4th Axis CNC adds a rotational axis (A-axis) to standard 3-axis machining. This enables intricate geometries, complex contours, and undercuts in a single setup — resulting in higher volume production at lower cost than multi-setup alternatives.
What materials do you machine?
We work with metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, and more), plastics, and composites. If you have a specific material requirement, include it in your RFQ and we'll confirm compatibility.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
Yes — we offer both rapid prototype development for design validation and full production runs via 4th Axis CNC. Our iterative prototype process is designed to transition seamlessly into production.
Do you offer CNC milling?
Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service at Mesquite Manufacturing. We offer multi-axis milling for complex geometries with tight tolerances across a wide range of materials.
Do you offer 3D printing?
Yes. Our additive manufacturing services achieve ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances across traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — suitable for demanding engineering applications.
How do I get a quote?
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, select your service and material, and optionally attach a drawing or CAD file. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. No obligation.
How fast can you turn around a project?
We operate 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead times depend on complexity, material, and volume — contact us with your project details and we'll provide a specific timeline in your quote.

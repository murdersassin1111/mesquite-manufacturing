KNOWLEDGE BASE

Frequently Asked Questions

◆

Common technical and process questions from engineers and procurement teams.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on CNC machining and milling operations, as well as our 3D printing services. For your specific part requirements, request a quote and we'll confirm achievable tolerances.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth axis (4th axis) CNC machining adds a rotational axis to traditional 3-axis machining. This enables us to create intricate geometries and complex features in a single setup — producing high-volume parts at lower cost than competitors.

What materials do you machine?

We work with a broad range of metals, plastics, and composites. Whether you need aluminum, steel, titanium, engineering plastics, or composite materials, we have the capability to machine it to your specifications.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Absolutely. We offer dedicated Prototype Development for rapid design validation and iterative refinement, as well as full production machining for high-volume runs — using our 4th axis CNC for cost-effective production.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — precision CNC milling is one of our core capabilities. Our milling services deliver high-quality components with exceptional precision and accuracy, suitable for both prototype and production quantities.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service produces parts from traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances — bringing machining-grade precision to 3D printing.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page with your part description, material, quantity, and tolerance requirements. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. The quote is completely free with no obligation.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We operate 24/7 to provide the best service possible. Lead time depends on part complexity and quantity. Contact us through the RFQ form with your timeline requirements and we'll confirm availability.

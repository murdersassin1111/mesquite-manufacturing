COMMON QUESTIONS

Technical answers for engineers and procurement teams.

Our CNC machining and milling processes achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ depending on material and geometry. Our 4th axis CNC systems maintain these tolerances even on complex multi-face geometries at high volume.
Fourth (4th) axis CNC adds rotational movement to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and undercuts without repositioning the part. This allows us to machine complex designs at high volume and lower cost than competitors limited to 3-axis systems.
We machine metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, titanium, brass, copper), plastics (ABS, nylon, HDPE, PEEK, Delrin), and composites (carbon fiber, fiberglass, G10). Contact us with your specific material for confirmation.
Yes — we offer both rapid prototype development (single parts, quick validation) and production CNC machining at volume. Our 4th axis systems are optimized for both low-count prototypes and high-volume production runs.
Yes — precision CNC milling is a core service. Our mills operate with 4th axis capability and achieve ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances on metals, plastics, and composites.
Yes — our additive manufacturing service achieves ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals. 3D printing is ideal for complex geometries, rapid prototyping, and low-volume specialized parts.

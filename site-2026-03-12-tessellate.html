FAQ

Engineer's Guide
to Working With Us

The questions we get most — answered precisely.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances across metals, plastics, and composites — meeting the demands of precision engineering applications.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds rotation to the standard 3-axis system, enabling intricate geometries in a single setup. This allows us to produce high-volume complex parts at lower cost than competitors.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites — covering the full spectrum from aluminum and steel to engineering plastics and advanced composite materials.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Absolutely. Our Prototype Development service rapidly validates designs, and we scale seamlessly into production machining runs using the same precision equipment and processes.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — precision CNC milling is a core service. Our milling operations deliver the same tight tolerances and surface finish quality as all our machining work.

Do you offer 3D printing with precision tolerances?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service produces parts to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in plastics, composites, and metals — comparable to traditional CNC output.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page with your project details. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week — no obligation, always free.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We operate 24/7. Lead time depends on complexity and volume — contact us with your specific project and we'll give you an accurate timeline in your quote.

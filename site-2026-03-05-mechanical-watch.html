Frequently Asked Questions

Technical answers for the engineers and buyers who need to know before they quote.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We hold tolerances from ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ depending on material, geometry, and process — among the tightest in the industry for both CNC machining and 3D printing.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth axis CNC adds rotational movement to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and multi-face features in a single setup. This allows us to produce high-volume complex parts at lower cost than competitors.

What materials do you machine?

We work across metals, plastics, and composites — including aluminum, steel, titanium, ABS, PEEK, carbon fiber composites, and more. Contact us with your material spec for confirmation.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — both. Our Prototype Development service is built for rapid iteration, while our CNC machining line handles high-volume production with consistent tolerances across every part.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — CNC milling is a core service. Precision CNC milling is included under our Precision Machining offering, with full 4th axis capability for complex milled geometries.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service prints in traditional plastics, composites, and metals to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances — production-grade, not hobbyist-grade.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page. Describe your part, upload a drawing or CAD file if available, and we'll respond with a tailored quote within 24 hours, seven days a week.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We work 24/7 and turnaround times depend on part complexity, quantity, and material. Submit your project details and we'll provide a lead time specific to your requirements.

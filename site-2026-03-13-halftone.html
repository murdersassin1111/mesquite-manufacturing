FAQ

Common Questions

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across our CNC machining, milling, and 3D printing services. Tighter tolerances are possible — submit your specs and we'll advise.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds a rotational axis (A-axis) to standard 3-axis machining. This enables intricate geometries, complex angles, and continuous contours — all at high volume with lower cost per part.

What materials do you machine?

We work with a wide range of metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, brass), engineering plastics, and advanced composites. Not sure about your material? Ask us — we'll advise on machinability.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Absolutely. Our prototype development service gets your design validated fast, and we scale seamlessly to full production runs using our 4th axis CNC platform for high-volume efficiency.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service at Mesquite Manufacturing. We offer multi-axis milling to achieve complex surface profiles and tight tolerances across all our materials.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service works with plastics, composites, and metals — hitting ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances. Great for complex geometries and fast-turn prototype parts.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form below with your project details and upload any drawings or CAD files. We respond within 24 hours — 7 days a week, including weekends.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7. Lead time depends on part complexity, quantity, and current queue. Submit your project specs and we'll provide a specific lead time in your quote.

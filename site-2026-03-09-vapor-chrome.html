// Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything engineers and procurement teams need to know before sending their first RFQ.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We machine to ±0.001″–0.005″ on standard CNC work, and our 3D printing matches that same precision range. Tighter tolerances are available for specific applications — describe your requirements in the RFQ form.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis machining, enabling complex, intricate geometries to be machined in a single setup at high volume — and at lower cost than multi-op alternatives. It's our key competitive advantage.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites across both CNC machining and 3D printing. Submit your project specs and we'll confirm material compatibility and recommend optimal processes.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — prototype development and full production runs are both core offerings. We can take a project from initial concept and first prototype all the way through high-volume production.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service. Combined with our 4th axis capability, we can mill complex geometries with exceptional accuracy across a wide range of materials.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes — additive manufacturing to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals. Ideal for rapid prototyping and complex geometries that are difficult to machine.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — include your part description, material, quantity, tolerance requirements, and any drawings. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead times depend on complexity, material, and volume. Contact us with your timeline and we'll confirm feasibility with your quote.

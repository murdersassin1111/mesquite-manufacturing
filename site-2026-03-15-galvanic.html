Frequently Asked Questions

Questions Engineers Ask

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
We achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances across our CNC machining, milling, and 3D printing services.
What is fourth axis CNC machining?
4th axis CNC adds rotational movement to the standard 3-axis setup — enabling intricate geometries at high volume with lower cost per part than traditional methods.
What materials do you machine?
We machine metals, plastics, and composites — covering the full spectrum of engineering materials across CNC machining, milling, and 3D printing.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
Yes — we offer dedicated Prototype Development services for rapid iteration, and full production CNC machining and milling at high volume.
Do you offer CNC milling?
Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service. Our 4th axis capability extends what's possible with milling for complex part geometries.
Do you offer 3D printing?
Yes — additive manufacturing with ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances across traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals.

How fast can you turn around a project?

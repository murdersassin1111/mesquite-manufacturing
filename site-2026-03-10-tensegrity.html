FAQ

Common Questions from Engineers

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on CNC machined parts. Our precision is consistent across metals, plastics, and composites — contact us for project-specific tolerance requirements.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds a rotational axis (A-axis) to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and undercuts in a single setup. The result: higher complexity at high volume, lower cost than multi-setup competitors.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, titanium, brass), plastics (PEEK, Delrin, nylon, ABS), and composites. Both CNC machining and 3D printing support this full material range.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Absolutely. We offer rapid prototype development for design validation, and scale directly to production volume with the same precision. No need to switch vendors when you move from prototype to production.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — precision CNC milling is one of our core services. We handle flat surfaces, contours, slots, and complex 3D profiles with our 4th axis machines for superior accuracy and efficiency.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service produces parts to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances. We print in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metal — ideal for complex geometries and rapid iterations.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page with your part description, material, and quantity. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. Upload a drawing or CAD file for the most accurate quote.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead times depend on part complexity and quantity — contact us with your project details and deadline, and we'll give you a specific timeline.

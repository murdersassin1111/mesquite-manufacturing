TECHNICAL NOTES // FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Engineering-focused answers to the questions we hear most often from customers evaluating Mesquite Manufacturing.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We hold tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on CNC machined and milled components, as well as on 3D printed parts. Your specific application and material will determine the best process.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds rotational movement (A-axis) to traditional 3-axis machining. This enables intricate geometries and undercuts in a single setup, producing high-volume parts with greater precision at lower cost than multi-setup conventional machining.

What materials do you machine?

We machine metals, plastics, and composites across our CNC machining, milling, and 3D printing services. Submit your RFQ with material specifications and we'll confirm capability and recommend the best process.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — both prototype development and full production runs are core offerings. We can start with a rapid prototype to validate your design and transition seamlessly into production volume machining.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is one of our primary services. We offer both standard 3-axis and advanced 4th axis milling for complex surface geometries and high-volume production.

Do you offer 3D printing services?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service supports traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals to tolerances of ±0.001″–0.005″ — matching our CNC precision standards.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page with your project details. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. The quote is free and there's no obligation to proceed.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7 and work to supply the best service possible. Lead time depends on part complexity, quantity, and material. Contact us with your specific requirements for an accurate timeline.

TECHNICAL Q&A

Frequently Asked Questions

Engineering answers to the questions we hear most.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
We achieve tolerances from ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ depending on material, geometry, and process. For the tightest requirements, our 4th axis CNC is the optimal choice.
What is fourth axis CNC machining?
4th axis CNC adds rotational motion to 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries on multiple faces in a single setup. This allows us to manufacture complex high-volume parts at lower cost than competitors.
What materials do you machine?
We work with metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, brass, and more), engineering plastics (PEEK, nylon, acetal, etc.), and advanced composites — across both CNC machining and 3D printing services.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
Absolutely. Our Prototype Development service is purpose-built for early-stage validation and iteration. Once your design is finalized, we scale to production machining without skipping a beat.
Do you offer CNC milling?
Yes — precision CNC milling is a core service. We mill metals, plastics, and composites to tight tolerances, with 4th axis capability for complex multi-face geometries.
Do you offer 3D printing?
Yes. Our additive manufacturing service produces parts in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances — bridging the gap between prototype and production.
How do I get a quote?
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, attach a drawing or CAD file if you have one, and we'll respond with a custom quote within 24 hours. Free, no obligation.
How fast can you turn around a project?
We're available 24/7 and work to supply the best service possible. Lead times depend on complexity, material, and quantity — contact us with your specifics for an accurate timeline.

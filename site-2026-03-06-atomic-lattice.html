Frequently Asked

Questions from Engineers Like You

Everything you need to know before submitting your RFQ.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
We routinely hold tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across CNC machining, CNC milling, and 3D printing operations.
What is fourth axis CNC machining?
4th Axis CNC adds rotational capability to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and undercuts at high volume — at lower cost than multi-setup machining.
What materials do you machine?
We work with metals, plastics, and composites across all services. Submit your RFQ with your material specs for a tailored recommendation.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
Yes — our Prototype Development service is built for rapid first-articles, and our CNC Machining service scales to high-volume production efficiently.
Do you offer CNC milling?
Absolutely. Precision CNC milling is a core service, delivered with 4th axis technology for exceptional accuracy and throughput.
Do you offer 3D printing?
Yes — additive manufacturing to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in plastics, composites, and metals.
How do I get a quote?
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, attach a drawing if you have one, and we'll respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week.
How fast can you turn around a project?
We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead time depends on project complexity — submit your RFQ and we'll give you a specific timeline.

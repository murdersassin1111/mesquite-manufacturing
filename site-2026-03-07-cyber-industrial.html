KNOWLEDGE BASE

Frequently Asked Questions

Technical answers to the questions engineers ask most about our CNC machining and manufacturing services.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We hold tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ depending on material, geometry, and process. Tight-tolerance applications use our precision CNC milling with full inspection.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th Axis CNC adds a rotational axis (A-axis) to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and undercuts at high volume. This allows us to manufacture complex parts at lower cost than competitors using only 3-axis equipment.

What materials do you machine?

We machine metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, titanium, brass, and more), engineering plastics (PEEK, Delrin, nylon, HDPE, etc.), and composite materials. Contact us about your specific material.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes. We offer rapid prototype development for early-stage validation and full production CNC machining runs. Many customers start with prototyping and scale to production with us.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service. Our 4th Axis milling centers allow us to tackle complex profiles, pockets, and geometries that standard mills cannot achieve.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service achieves ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — ideal for prototypes and end-use parts alike.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — include your part description, quantity, material, and tolerances. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week, with a custom quote tailored to your project.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead times depend on part complexity, quantity, and material. Submit your RFQ and we'll include lead time in your custom quote.

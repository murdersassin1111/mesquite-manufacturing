Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
We routinely hold tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across our CNC machining and milling operations, and our 3D printing service achieves the same range for additive parts.
What is fourth axis CNC machining?
4th axis CNC adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and complex features in a single setup. This translates to higher precision, shorter cycle times, and lower cost per part at high volume.
What materials do you machine?
We work with metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, brass, and more), engineering plastics, and advanced composites. If you have a specific material requirement, contact us — we'll confirm compatibility.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
Absolutely. Our Prototype Development service handles first articles and design validation, while our Precision Machining capability scales to high-volume production runs — same quality standards throughout.
Do you offer CNC milling?
Yes — precision CNC milling is a core capability. Combined with our 4th axis technology, we machine complex 3D profiles and contoured surfaces with exceptional accuracy.
Do you offer 3D printing?
Yes. Our additive manufacturing service covers traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — with tolerances of ±0.001″–0.005″. Ideal for rapid prototypes and complex end-use geometries.

How fast can you turn around a project?

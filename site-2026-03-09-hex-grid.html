FAQ — Engineer Answers

Straight answers to the questions engineers ask before submitting an RFQ.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
We machine to ±0.001″ – ±0.005″ across metals, plastics, and composites. Our 4th Axis CNC equipment is calibrated for tight-tolerance work on complex geometries.
What is fourth axis CNC machining?
4th Axis CNC adds rotational movement to traditional 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and undercuts in a single setup. This means higher complexity at higher volume — and lower cost than multi-setup alternatives.
What materials do you machine?
We work with a wide range: metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, and more), plastics, and composites — across both CNC machining and 3D printing services.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
Yes. We offer dedicated Prototype Development for fast design validation as well as high-volume CNC production. The same precision standards apply at every scale.
Do you offer CNC milling?
Yes — precision CNC milling is one of our core services. We operate multi-axis CNC milling centers capable of tight-tolerance work across a wide range of materials and part geometries.
Do you offer 3D printing?
Yes. Our additive manufacturing service prints traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals to ±0.001″ – ±0.005″ tolerances — engineering-grade precision.
How do I get a quote?
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — it takes 2 minutes. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. No obligation, no spam. Just a detailed quote for your project.
How fast can you turn around a project?
We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead times depend on complexity, quantity, and material — contact us with your specs and timeline and we'll give you a straight answer.

Common Questions

Everything You Need
Before Requesting a Quote

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across both CNC machining/milling and 3D printing operations — some of the tightest tolerances in the industry.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds rotational movement to standard 3-axis machining, enabling complex, intricate geometries in a single setup. This allows us to produce high-volume parts at lower cost than competitors using conventional methods.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, and more), plastics, and composite materials. Contact us about your specific material requirements.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes. We offer both rapid prototype development for design validation and full production machining. Many customers start with a prototype and scale to production with us.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — precision CNC milling is one of our core services. We combine milling with our 4th axis capability for highly complex parts at scale.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service works with traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, upload a drawing if you have one, and select your service and material. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7 and work around the clock to deliver. Lead time depends on part complexity, material, and volume — contact us with your project details for a specific timeline.

FAQ

Precision Answers

Common questions from engineers and procurement teams about our CNC machining capabilities.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We routinely hold tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across all our CNC machining, milling, and 3D printing services.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth axis CNC adds rotational movement (A-axis) to traditional 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and complex contours in a single setup — at high volume with lower cost than multi-setup alternatives.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites. Contact us with your specific material requirements and we'll confirm capabilities for your project.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — prototype development and production runs are both core services. We support the full product lifecycle from initial concept validation through high-volume manufacturing.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes, precision CNC milling is a core service. Our 4th axis milling capability allows for complex geometries at high volume with exceptional accuracy.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes, our additive manufacturing service produces parts to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances using traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We operate 24/7 and work to deliver the best service possible. Lead time depends on part complexity and quantity — contact us with your timeline and we'll provide a specific commitment.

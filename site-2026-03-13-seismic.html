Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before submitting your RFQ.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We routinely machine to ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ depending on the part geometry, material, and process. Our 4th axis CNC equipment and skilled operators maintain those tolerances consistently across prototype and production quantities.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds rotational movement to standard 3-axis machining, enabling complex geometries and undercuts in a single setup. This reduces cost, improves accuracy, and allows us to manufacture high-volume parts at lower cost than competitors using conventional setups.

What materials do you machine?

We work across a broad range of materials including metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, brass, titanium), plastics (PEEK, Delrin, nylon, HDPE), and composites. Not sure what you need? We can advise on material selection.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — both. Our Prototype Development service is purpose-built for early-stage validation, while our Precision Machining capability scales to high-volume production. Many customers prototype with us and then move directly into production.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service. We operate multi-axis CNC mills capable of complex contoured surfaces, slots, pockets, and features to tight dimensional tolerances across metals, plastics, and composites.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing capability covers traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — all held to the same exacting standards as our CNC processes: ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form below — describe your part, upload a drawing if available, and we'll respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. No obligation, no cost to quote.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead time depends on your specific project's complexity, material, and quantity. Contact us with your details and we'll provide a realistic timeline upfront.

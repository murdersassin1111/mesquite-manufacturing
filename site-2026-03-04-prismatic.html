FAQ

Questions From Engineers

Straightforward answers to the questions procurement teams and engineers ask most.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
We hold tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on both CNC machined and 3D printed parts — some of the tightest in the industry for production-volume work.
What is fourth axis CNC machining?
Fourth axis (4th axis) CNC adds rotational movement around the A-axis, enabling intricate geometries, wrapped features, and complex multi-face parts in a single setup — at high volume and lower cost than multi-setup conventional machining.
What materials do you machine?
We machine metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, brass, and more), engineering plastics, and composite materials. Our 3D printing also covers traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metal powders.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
Yes — we offer dedicated prototype development services for design validation, and scale seamlessly to high-volume production using our 4th axis CNC capability. Same quality standards, either volume.
Do you offer CNC milling?
Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service at Mesquite Manufacturing. We perform 3-axis and 4th-axis milling operations to produce complex contours, pockets, slots, and surface profiles to tight tolerances.
Do you offer 3D printing?
Yes. Our additive manufacturing services achieve ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in plastics, composites, and metals — ideal for complex geometries, internal channels, and rapid part iterations.
How do I get a quote?
Fill out the RFQ form on this page. Describe your part, attach a drawing or CAD file if you have one, and we'll respond with a tailored quote within 24 hours — 7 days a week, including weekends.
How fast can you turn around a project?
We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead times vary by project complexity and quantity. Submit your RFQ and we'll provide a specific timeline tailored to your part and delivery requirements.

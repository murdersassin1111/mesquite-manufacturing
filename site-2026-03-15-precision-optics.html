FAQ

Questions Engineers Ask

Everything you need to know before submitting your RFQ.

Q_01 //
What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We regularly hold ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances — both on CNC machining/milling and 3D printing. For your specific tolerance requirements, include them in your RFQ.

Q_02 //
What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds a rotation axis to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and complex features that would require multiple setups otherwise. This means faster production, lower cost, and better accuracy for your parts.

Q_03 //
What materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites. This includes aluminum, steel, titanium, engineering plastics, and innovative composite materials. Tell us your material in the RFQ form.

Q_04 //
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — we offer both rapid prototype development for design validation and full production machining runs. Our 4th axis CNC is especially suited for high-volume production at lower cost.

Q_05 //
Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — precision CNC milling is one of our core services. We offer 3-axis and 4th axis CNC milling on metals, plastics, and composites to ±0.001″ tolerances.

Q_06 //
Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes — additive manufacturing to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals. Ideal for complex geometries and fast design iterations.

Q_07 //
How do I get a quote?

Fill out our RFQ form below — describe your part, material, quantity, and tolerance requirements. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. It's free and no obligation.

Q_08 //
How fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7 to keep your project moving. Lead times depend on complexity and volume. Contact us through the RFQ form and we'll give you a specific timeline for your project.

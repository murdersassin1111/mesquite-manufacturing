// FAQ

Common Questions

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We consistently achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across our CNC machining and 3D printing services. For critical applications requiring tighter specs, contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth axis (4th axis) CNC machining adds a rotational A-axis to standard 3-axis machines, enabling intricate geometries and multi-face machining without repositioning the part. This allows us to manufacture complex, high-volume parts at lower cost than competitors using traditional setups.

What materials do you machine?

We work with a broad range of materials including metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, and more), plastics, and composites. Both our CNC machining and 3D printing services support this full material range.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — absolutely. Our Prototype Development service is specifically designed for rapid concept validation, while our Precision CNC Machining service scales to high-volume production runs using 4th axis technology to keep unit costs low.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes, precision CNC milling is a core part of our machining service. We perform high-accuracy milling operations on metals, plastics, and composites with 4th axis capability for complex geometries.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service produces parts to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances using traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — delivering production-grade quality.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, upload any drawings, and select your service. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. There's no obligation and no cost for the quote.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We operate 24/7 and work to meet your timeline. Lead time depends on part complexity, material availability, and volume. Submit your RFQ and mention your deadline — we'll give you an honest schedule upfront.

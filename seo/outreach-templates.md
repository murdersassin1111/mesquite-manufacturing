## Template 2: Guest Post Pitch (Manufacturing Trade Blog)

**Use for:** The Fabricator, Modern Machine Shop, Manufacturing Tomorrow, SME publications

**Subject:** Guest post idea: [Specific Topic] for [Publication Name]

```
Hi [Editor's Name],

I'm [Your Name], [Title] at Mesquite Manufacturing — a precision CNC 
machining company in Mesquite, TX specializing in 4th axis CNC and 
prototype development.

I've been a regular reader of [Publication] and noticed you haven't 
covered [specific topic gap] in depth recently. I'd love to contribute 
a practical guide that your readers — engineers and procurement managers 
— would find genuinely useful.

Proposed title: [e.g., "Design for Manufacturability: 10 CNC Rules 
That Cut Part Costs by 30%"]

The piece would cover:
• [Key point 1]
• [Key point 2]
• [Key point 3]

~1,800 words, original content, no sales pitch. I can include original 
diagrams or photos from our shop floor if useful.

Do you accept guest contributors? Happy to send a full draft or outline 
first — whatever works for your process.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
[Title] | Mesquite Manufacturing
[Phone] | [Email]
mesquitemanufacturing.com
```

**Tips:**
- Research the editor by name (LinkedIn, publication masthead)
- Reference a specific recent article you enjoyed
- Propose 2-3 topic options — let them choose
- Follow up once after 2 weeks if no response

## Template 3: Link Reclamation (Unlinked Brand Mentions)

**Use when:** You find a site mentioning "Mesquite Manufacturing" without linking to you.  
**Tool to find mentions:** Google Alerts for "Mesquite Manufacturing", Ahrefs Alerts.

**Subject:** Quick note about your mention of Mesquite Manufacturing

```
Hi [Name],

I came across your [article/page] at [URL] — thanks for the mention 
of Mesquite Manufacturing!

One small ask: would you be able to link the mention to our website 
(https://mesquitemanufacturing.com)? It helps readers find us directly 
and would really mean a lot to our small team.

Totally fine if it's not possible — just wanted to ask. Thanks either 
way for the coverage!

Best,
[Your Name]
Mesquite Manufacturing
```

**Notes:** Keep it short. No lengthy justification. Most people will say yes if it takes 30 seconds to do.

**Use for:** Mesquite Chamber of Commerce, Dallas Regional Chamber, local BNI groups

```
options with [Chamber Name].

Specifically, I'm interested in:
• Member directory listing and backlink from your website
• Networking events with local industrial businesses
• Co-marketing or sponsorship opportunities
• Ribbon cutting or new member announcements

Who is the best person to speak with about membership? 

Thanks in advance,
[Your Name]
[Title] | Mesquite Manufacturing
[Phone] | [Email]
mesquitemanufacturing.com
```

**Notes:**
- Chamber memberships typically cost $200–$800/year for SMBs
- The directory listing + local press = worth it for citations alone
- Ask specifically about backlink from their member directory
- Attend 2–3 networking events after joining — relationships = referrals

## ThomasNet-Specific Submission Notes

ThomasNet is the #1 industrial directory and requires more detail:

**Info to prepare:**
- Company description (500 words)
- Product/service categories (be specific — they have a taxonomy)
- Annual revenue range
- Number of employees
- Year founded
- Certifications (ISO 9001, AS9100, ITAR, etc.) — list if applicable
- Industries served
- Geographic scope (local, national, international)
- Minimum order quantity / lot size
- Lead time ranges
- CAD file formats accepted

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything engineers want to know before submitting their first RFQ.

Q What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across our CNC machining and 3D printing services — some of the tightest available in the industry.

Q What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and complex part features at high production volume — at a lower cost than traditional multi-setup machining.

Q What materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites. Contact us with your specific material requirements and we'll confirm suitability for your project.

Q Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — we offer dedicated Prototype Development services for rapid iteration and full production CNC machining runs for volume orders. One shop, both phases.

Q Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service. Our 4th axis milling capability lets us produce complex milled parts that would otherwise require multiple setups.

Q Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes — additive manufacturing to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals. Ideal for complex geometries and prototypes.

Q How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page with your project details, material, and quantity. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week — including weekends.

Q How fast can you turn around a project?

We operate 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead times depend on your specific part complexity and quantity — contact us for an accurate estimate on your project.

FAQ

Common Questions Answered

Everything engineers ask before submitting an RFQ — straight, factual answers.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
We achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on CNC machined parts and ±0.001″–0.005″ on 3D printed components — among the tightest tolerances available in contract manufacturing.
What is fourth axis CNC machining?
4th axis CNC adds a rotational axis (typically A-axis) to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries on complex shapes in a single setup — delivering higher volume output at lower cost per part than multi-setup alternatives.
What materials do you machine?
We machine metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, and more), engineering plastics, and composites — across both CNC machining and 3D printing services.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
Absolutely. Our Prototype Development service is specifically designed for rapid design validation, while our CNC Machining service scales to production volume with 4th axis efficiency.
Do you offer CNC milling?
Yes — precision CNC milling is a core capability within our Precision Machining service. We offer 3-axis and 4th axis CNC milling for a wide range of geometries and materials.
Do you offer 3D printing?
Yes. Our 3D Printing service provides additive manufacturing excellence in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — achieving ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances.
How do I get a quote?
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, upload your drawing if available, and select your service and material. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. It's free and no obligation.
How fast can you turn around a project?
We work 24/7 to provide the fastest service possible. Lead times depend on part complexity, material, and quantity. Contact us with your project specifics for an accurate timeline.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything engineers and procurement teams want to know before submitting an RFQ.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
We routinely achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances across CNC machining, milling, and 3D printing. Our 4th axis CNC equipment handles the tightest geometries at production scale.
What is fourth axis CNC machining?
Fourth axis CNC adds rotational movement (A-axis) to the standard X/Y/Z, enabling complex geometries and intricate designs without repositioning. This allows us to produce high-volume parts at significantly lower cost than multi-setup alternatives.
What materials do you machine?
We work with metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, titanium, and more), plastics, and composites. Tell us what your application requires and we'll confirm material availability and optimal process selection.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
Yes — prototype development and full production runs are both core services. We can take you from first prototype through iterative refinement and into high-volume machining without switching vendors.
Do you offer CNC milling?
CNC milling is a core service. Our precision milling capabilities cover 3-axis and 4th axis work, handling everything from simple prismatic parts to complex contoured surfaces across metals, plastics, and composites.
Do you offer 3D printing?
Yes. Our additive manufacturing service achieves ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — the same precision standard we apply to all our CNC work.
How do I get a quote?
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, attach a drawing if you have one, and tell us your material, quantity, and timeline. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week, no obligation.
How fast can you turn around a project?
We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead time depends on part complexity, quantity, and material. Contact us with your project details and we'll give you a specific timeline in your quote.

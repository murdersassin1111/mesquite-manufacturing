FAQ

Common Questions

Everything engineers need to know before submitting an RFQ.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across our CNC machining and milling services. Our 4th axis technology and skilled team maintain these tolerances even on complex, high-volume production runs.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth axis CNC adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis machining. This enables us to machine intricate geometries at high volume in a single setup — producing parts at lower cost than competitors while maintaining tight tolerances.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites. Whether you need aluminum, steel, titanium, engineering plastics, or advanced composite materials, our team has the expertise and tooling to handle it.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — we offer both Prototype Development for rapid concept validation and full production CNC machining for high-volume runs. Many clients start with a prototype and scale directly to production with us.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is one of our core services. We use advanced milling techniques with 4th axis capability to produce complex parts with exceptional surface finish and tight dimensional accuracy.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our 3D printing / additive manufacturing service achieves tolerances of ±0.001″–0.005″ using traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals. Great for complex geometries and rapid prototyping.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — it takes under 2 minutes. Include your part description, material, quantity, and tolerance requirements. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week, with a tailored quote.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7 and work to supply the best service possible. Lead time depends on your project's complexity and volume. Submit your RFQ and we'll include a timeline estimate in your custom quote.

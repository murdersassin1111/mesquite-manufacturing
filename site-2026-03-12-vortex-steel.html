Common Questions from Engineers

Technical answers for technical people. If you don't see your question, reach out — we respond 24/7.

We achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances on our CNC machining and milling operations. Our 3D printing service matches those same tolerances. Specific achievable tolerance depends on geometry, material, and feature size — contact us with your drawing for a precise assessment.
Fourth axis CNC machining adds a rotational axis (A-axis) to standard 3-axis movement. This enables cutting around a cylindrical or curved workpiece without manual repositioning — allowing intricate geometries to be machined at high volume and significantly lower cost than multi-setup alternatives. It's a key differentiator that lets Mesquite Manufacturing out-compete standard shops on complex parts.
We machine metals, plastics, and composites across all our services — CNC machining, CNC milling, and 3D printing. Contact us with your specific material and we'll confirm compatibility and recommend the optimal process.

Yes — precision CNC milling is one of our core services. We mill metals, plastics, and composites to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances, with 4th axis capability for complex geometries that standard 3-axis milling cannot achieve efficiently.

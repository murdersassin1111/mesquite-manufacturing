Frequently Asked

Engineer's FAQ

Everything you need to know before submitting your RFQ.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
We achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances across both CNC machining and 3D printing services, depending on material and geometry.
What is fourth axis CNC machining?
4th Axis CNC adds rotational movement to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and complex contours at high volume — and at lower cost than competitors.
What materials do you machine?
We machine metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, and more), plastics, and composites — across all four of our service lines.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
Yes. Our Prototype Development service handles iterative design validation, while our Precision CNC Machining handles high-volume production using 4th Axis technology.
Do you offer CNC milling?
Yes — precision CNC milling is a core capability, part of our Precision Machining service with ±0.001″ tolerances and 4th Axis technology.
Do you offer 3D printing?
Yes. Our additive manufacturing service achieves ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances using traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals.
How do I get a quote?
Submit the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, material, quantity, and timeline. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week.
How fast can you turn around a project?
We're available 24/7. Lead time depends on your specific project complexity and volume — contact us via the RFQ form and we'll provide a tailored timeline.

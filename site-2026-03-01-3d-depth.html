// Common Questions

ENGINEERS ASK US

The questions we hear most — answered straight.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
We routinely machine to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances. Our 4th Axis CNC equipment and rigorous quality assurance process ensures those tolerances are held consistently across every part in a run.
What is fourth axis CNC machining?
4th Axis CNC adds a rotational axis (A-axis) to standard 3-axis milling, enabling machining of complex, intricate geometries in a single setup. This dramatically reduces cost on high-volume runs while expanding design freedom.
What materials do you machine?
We work with metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, titanium, and more), plastics, and composites. Our 3D printing service also handles traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metal materials.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
Absolutely. Prototype Development and Precision Machining are both core offerings. We can take a design from first prototype through iterative refinement and into full high-volume production — all under one roof.
Do you offer CNC milling?
Yes — precision CNC milling is one of our core services. From simple 3-axis milling to complex 4th Axis operations, we handle the full range of milling requirements for any industry.
Do you offer 3D printing?
Yes. Our additive manufacturing service delivers parts to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in plastics, composites, and metal materials. Great for complex geometries, rapid prototypes, or parts that are difficult to machine.
How do I get a quote?
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — takes under 2 minutes. Include your part description, material, tolerance, and timeline. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. Free quote, no obligation.
How fast can you turn around a project?
We work 24/7 to deliver the best service possible. Lead times depend on complexity, quantity, and material. Submit your RFQ and we'll provide a specific timeline with your custom quote — usually within 24 hours.

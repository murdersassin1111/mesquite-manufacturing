Common Questions

FAQ — Straight Answers

Everything engineers ask before sending their first RFQ. Don't see your question? Ask us directly.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We hold tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across our CNC machining and 3D printing services. Tighter tolerances are available for specific applications — include your requirements in your RFQ.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries at high volume. This allows us to produce complex parts at lower cost than competitors using only 3-axis setups.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites. This includes aluminum, steel, titanium, brass, engineering plastics, and advanced composite materials. Specify your material in the RFQ form.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — both are core services. Our Prototype Development service is built for rapid iteration. Once your design is validated, we scale to full production using our 4th axis CNC and 3D printing capabilities.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is one of our core offerings. We perform multi-axis milling operations on metals, plastics, and composites with exceptional accuracy and surface finish.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service covers traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — all to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances. 3D printing is ideal for complex geometries and low-volume production.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page. Include your part description, material, quantity, tolerance, and any drawings or CAD files. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week — no obligation.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We operate 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead time depends on part complexity, quantity, and material. Include your timeline in the RFQ and we'll confirm what's achievable for your project.

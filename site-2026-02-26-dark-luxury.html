FAQ

Everything engineers and procurement managers need to know.

Q → What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We routinely achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances — on both CNC machined and 3D printed parts. Our 4th axis capability allows us to hold precision on complex geometries.

Q → What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth axis CNC adds rotational movement to standard 3-axis machining — enabling intricate, multi-sided geometries in a single setup. This dramatically reduces cost on high-volume runs compared to conventional methods.

Q → What materials do you machine?

We work across metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, and more), plastics, and composites. If you're unsure about material selection, our team can advise on the best fit for your application.

Q → Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — we specialize in both. From single prototypes for design validation to high-volume production runs, our workflow handles the full lifecycle of your part.

Q → Do you offer CNC milling?

Precision CNC milling is one of our core services. We offer multi-axis milling with tight tolerances, suitable for complex part geometries in a wide range of materials.

Q → Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service achieves ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances using traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — ideal for prototypes and end-use parts.

Q → How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form below with your project details. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week — with a tailored quote including material and process recommendations.

Q → How fast can you turn around a project?

We operate 24/7. Lead time depends on part complexity and quantity — contact us with your project details and timeline and we'll work to meet your deadline.

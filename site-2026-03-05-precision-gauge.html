Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We routinely achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on CNC milled and turned parts. Our 4th Axis CNC technology allows us to maintain those tolerances even on complex, multi-face geometries.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th Axis CNC adds a rotational axis (A-axis) to standard 3-axis machining. This enables intricate, multi-sided geometries to be machined in a single setup — delivering higher volume output at lower per-part cost than conventional machining.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, brass, titanium), engineering plastics (ABS, Delrin, PEEK, nylon), and composites. If you're unsure about material selection, we're happy to advise based on your application requirements.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — prototype development and production machining are both core services. We help validate your design with a prototype first, then scale to high-volume production with the same precision and quality standards.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Precision CNC milling is one of our core capabilities. We produce complex milled components across metals, plastics, and composites — from simple profiles to intricate multi-axis parts.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service produces parts in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances. 3D printing is available for prototypes and short-run production parts.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, quantity, material, and timeline. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. You can also attach a drawing or CAD file directly in the form.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead times depend on part complexity, quantity, and material. Contact us with your project details and we'll give you a specific timeline in your quote.

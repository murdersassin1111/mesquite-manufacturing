FAQ

Common Questions

Everything engineers and buyers ask before their first RFQ.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve? +
We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across our CNC machining and 3D printing services — consistent across metals, plastics, and composites.
What is fourth axis CNC machining? +
Fourth axis (4th axis) CNC machining adds a rotational axis to the standard 3-axis setup, enabling intricate geometries and undercuts in a single setup. This reduces cycle time and cost, making it ideal for high-volume complex parts at lower cost than competitors using multiple setups.
What materials do you machine? +
We work with a wide range of materials including metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, titanium, and more), plastics, and composites. Contact us with your specific material and we'll confirm compatibility.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs? +
Yes — we offer both rapid prototype development and high-volume production runs. Many customers start with a prototype for design validation, then scale to production with us.
Do you offer CNC milling? +
Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service. Our milling capabilities include both standard 3-axis and fourth axis milling for complex geometries.
Do you offer 3D printing? +
Yes. Our 3D printing service achieves ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances using traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — ideal for complex geometries that traditional machining can't reach economically.
How do I get a quote? +
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, upload a drawing if available, and select your service. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. It's free and there's no obligation.
How fast can you turn around a project? +
We're available 24/7 and work to supply the best service possible. Lead time depends on your specific project complexity and quantity. Contact us with your details and we'll provide a timeline with your quote.

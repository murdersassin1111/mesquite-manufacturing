Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on both CNC machining and 3D printing operations. Our 4th Axis CNC technology maintains this precision even on complex geometries and high-volume production runs.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th Axis CNC adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries without repositioning the part. This allows us to manufacture complex components at high volume and lower cost than competitors using conventional methods.

What materials do you machine?

We machine metals, plastics, and composites. Whether you need aluminum, steel, titanium, engineering plastics, or advanced composite materials — our equipment and team are equipped to handle it.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes. We offer prototype development for rapid concept validation and iterative refinement, as well as full production CNC machining runs. The same precision standards apply to every quantity.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — precision CNC milling is a core service. Our CNC milling operations deliver the same ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances with full 4th axis capability for complex part geometries.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing services deliver ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances across traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — ideal for complex geometries and rapid iteration.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — include your part description, material, quantity, and tolerance requirements. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. No obligation, no cost.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We operate 24/7 to deliver the fastest possible turnaround. Lead time depends on part complexity, quantity, and material. Contact us with your project details and timeline for a specific answer.

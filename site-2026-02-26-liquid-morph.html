FAQ

Common Questions,
Straight Answers

Everything engineers and buyers ask before submitting their first RFQ.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We routinely achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across our CNC machining and 3D printing services — tight enough for critical aerospace, industrial, and medical-grade components.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds rotational movement (A-axis) to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries at high volume — faster cycle times and lower cost per part than traditional setups.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites across all services — from aluminum, steel, and titanium to engineering plastics and advanced composite materials.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes. Our Prototype Development service handles rapid iteration and design validation, while our CNC machining scales to high-volume production with consistent quality.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — precision CNC milling is a core service. We offer both 3-axis and 4th axis milling for complex geometries, high-volume components, and tight-tolerance applications.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service achieves ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances using traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — ideal alongside or instead of machined parts.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7 and work to your timeline. Lead times vary by complexity and volume — contact us with your deadline and we'll build a plan around it.

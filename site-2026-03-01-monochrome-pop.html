Common Questions

FAQ — Answered.

Everything engineers and buyers ask before placing an order.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We routinely achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on both CNC machined parts and 3D printed components — across metals, plastics, and composites.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th Axis CNC adds a rotational axis to traditional 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and complex contours at high volume — at lower cost than our competitors.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, and more), plastics, and composites. If you have a specific material requirement, ask us — we'll let you know what's possible.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Absolutely. Our Prototype Development service is designed for rapid iteration, and our Precision Machining scales to high-volume production — with no gap between the two.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service. We operate modern multi-axis mills with full 4th axis capability for complex part geometries.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service handles traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — at ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week — with a tailored quote, no obligation required.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7 and work to supply the best service as quickly as possible. Contact us with your project details for a specific lead-time estimate.

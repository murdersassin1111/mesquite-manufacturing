FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Technical answers for engineers, procurement teams, and project managers.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across our CNC machining and 3D printing services — suitable for high-precision aerospace, industrial, and mechanical applications.
What is fourth axis CNC machining?
4th axis CNC adds rotational movement to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and complex features at high volume — at lower cost than multi-setup conventional machining.
What materials do you machine?
We work with metals (steel, aluminum, brass, titanium, and more), engineering plastics, and advanced composite materials — across both CNC machining and 3D printing.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
Yes — we offer dedicated Prototype Development for design validation, and full production CNC machining via our 4th axis capability for high-volume runs.
Do you offer CNC milling?
Yes, precision CNC milling is a core service. Our 4th axis milling capability allows for complex contours and multi-face features in a single setup.
Do you offer 3D printing?
Yes. Our additive manufacturing service delivers precision 3D printing to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances using plastics, composites, and metal materials.
How do I get a quote?
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — we respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. Include your part description, quantity, material, and tolerance requirements for the fastest response.
How fast can you turn around a project?
We operate 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead times depend on project complexity and volume — contact us with your spec for a precise timeline.

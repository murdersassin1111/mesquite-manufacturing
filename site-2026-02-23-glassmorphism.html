FAQ

Common Questions About
CNC Machining & Milling

Everything engineers and procurement managers need to know before submitting an RFQ.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

Our precision CNC machining services achieve tolerances ranging from ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ — depending on material and geometry. We handle tight-tolerance parts for demanding applications across metals, plastics, and composites.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth axis (4th axis) CNC machining adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis milling, enabling machining of complex geometries without repositioning. This allows us to produce intricate parts at high volume and lower cost than competitors using standard 3-axis setups.

What materials do you work with?

We work with a wide range of materials including metals (steel, aluminum, and more), engineering plastics, and advanced composites. Our CNC machining and 3D printing capabilities cover the full spectrum of material requirements.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — both. Our prototype development service rapidly brings your concept to life for validation and refinement. Once you're ready, we scale to full production runs using our 4th axis CNC capabilities for high-volume, cost-effective output.

Do you offer CNC milling specifically?

Yes. CNC milling is a core part of our precision machining services. We utilize advanced fourth axis CNC milling to machine complex geometries with tight tolerances across all material types.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our 3D printing / additive manufacturing service produces top-tier components with tolerances as fine as ±0.001″–0.005″. We work with traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals for a broad range of applications.

How do I get a quote for my CNC project?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, material, quantity, tolerance requirements, and timeline. We review every submission and respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We are available 24/7 and work hard to accommodate your timeline. Contact us with your project details and we will provide a specific lead time estimate based on complexity, quantity, and service type.

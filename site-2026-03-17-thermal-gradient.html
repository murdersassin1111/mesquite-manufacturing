Common Questions

Engineer's FAQ

Everything you need to know before sending your RFQ.

QWhat CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We regularly hold ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances across our CNC machining, milling, and 3D printing services. Contact us with your specific requirements and we'll confirm capability before quoting.

QWhat is fourth axis (4th axis) CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds rotational capability to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and undercuts in a single setup. This allows us to manufacture complex, high-volume parts at lower cost than our competitors.

QWhat materials do you work with?

We machine and print metals, plastics, and composites. Whether it's aluminum, stainless steel, engineering plastics, or advanced composites, we have the tooling and expertise for your material.

QCan you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — our prototype development service brings concepts to life quickly for design validation, and we scale seamlessly to high-volume production using 4th axis CNC and other technologies. One partner from concept to production.

QDo you offer CNC milling?

Absolutely. Precision CNC milling is a core service. We produce high-quality milled components across metals, plastics, and composites with exceptional accuracy and surface finish.

QDo you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service achieves ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances using traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — precision on par with our CNC services.

QHow do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page. Describe your part, attach any drawings or CAD files, select your material and service, and submit. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week.

QHow fast can you turn around a project?

We operate 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead time depends on complexity, quantity, and material — contact us with your project details and we'll provide a specific timeline in your quote.

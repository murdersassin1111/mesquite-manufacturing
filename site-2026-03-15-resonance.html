FAQ

Common Questions About
Our CNC Machining Services

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve? ▶
We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on both CNC machining and 3D printing operations. For critical applications requiring the tightest tolerances, contact us to discuss your specific requirements.
What is fourth axis CNC machining? ▶
Fourth axis (4th axis) CNC adds rotational movement to standard 3-axis machining. This enables intricate geometries, undercuts, and complex contours that are impossible on 3-axis machines — and allows us to manufacture high-volume parts at lower cost than competitors.
What materials do you machine? ▶
We work with a full range of materials including metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, titanium, brass, copper), plastics (ABS, Delrin, PEEK, nylon, polycarbonate), and composites. Contact us for material-specific questions.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs? ▶
Yes — we offer both rapid prototype development for design validation and full production CNC machining runs. Our 4th axis technology makes us especially cost-competitive at higher volumes.
Do you offer CNC milling? ▶
Yes. Precision CNC milling is one of our core services. We use advanced multi-axis milling centers to produce complex shapes, pockets, slots, and contours with exceptional accuracy.
Do you offer 3D printing? ▶
Yes. Our additive manufacturing (3D printing) service handles traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals to tolerances of ±0.001″–±0.005″. We use 3D printing for rapid prototyping and production of complex geometries.
How do I get a quote? ▶
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — we respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. Include your part description, material, quantity, tolerance requirements, and any drawings or CAD files. The quote is free with no obligation.
How fast can you turn around a project? ▶
We're available 24/7 and work continuously to deliver your parts as quickly as possible. Lead times vary based on complexity, quantity, and materials. Contact us with your specific project details and timeline for an accurate delivery estimate.

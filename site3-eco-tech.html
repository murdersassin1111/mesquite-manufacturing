Sustainability Roadmap

Our 2030 Green Commitment

We've set public, audited targets. Here's how we're getting there — and where we stand today.

☀️

100% Renewable Energy

Our 2.4MW rooftop solar array and wind energy PPAs power the entire facility. Achieved in 2024 — two years ahead of schedule.

Zero Waste to Landfill

All metal scrap, coolants, packaging, and process byproducts are recycled, repurposed, or energy-recovered. Target: 100% by 2026.

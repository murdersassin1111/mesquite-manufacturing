What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
We routinely achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across both CNC machining and 3D printing services. For ultra-tight tolerance requirements, discuss your specs with us and we'll confirm capability upfront.
What is fourth axis (4th axis) CNC machining?
4th axis CNC adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries and complex features in a single setup. This allows high-volume production at lower cost than competitors requiring multiple operations.
What materials do you machine?
We work with metals (steel, aluminum, titanium, brass, etc.), plastics (ABS, nylon, PEEK, etc.), and composites. Contact us with your specific material — we'll confirm compatibility and recommend the best process.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
Yes — we offer both Prototype Development (rapid iteration, cost-effective validation) and full production CNC machining at volume. Many customers prototype with us, then move directly to production.
Do you offer CNC milling?
Yes. Precision CNC milling is one of our core capabilities, delivering tight tolerances and excellent surface finish across a wide range of materials and geometries.
Do you offer 3D printing?
Yes — our additive manufacturing service achieves ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances across traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals. It's ideal for complex geometries and rapid iterations.
How do I get a quote?
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — include your part description, material, quantity, tolerance, and any drawings. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week, with a tailored quote.
How fast can you turn around a project?
We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead time depends on part complexity, quantity, and material — submit your RFQ and we'll include a timeline in your quote.

// Common Questions

FREQUENTLY
ASKED

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve? ▾
We achieve tolerances from ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ depending on geometry, material, and process. Both CNC machining and our 3D printing services can hit this range. For tight-tolerance work, we recommend discussing your requirements before quoting.
What is fourth axis (4th axis) CNC machining? ▾
Fourth axis CNC machining adds a rotational axis (A-axis) to the standard 3-axis setup, enabling a single setup to machine complex geometries, helical features, and curved surfaces that would otherwise require multiple setups. This means higher accuracy, lower cost, and faster cycle times for complex parts.
What materials do you machine? ▾
We work with a broad range of materials including metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, brass, titanium), plastics (PEEK, Delrin, nylon, ABS, polycarbonate), and composites. Our 3D printing also covers innovative composite materials.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs? ▾
Yes — we offer both Prototype Development (rapid, iterative, low quantities) and production CNC machining. Many customers start with a prototype and transition to full production with us once their design is validated.
Do you offer CNC milling? ▾
Yes, precision CNC milling is a core service. We perform 3-axis and 4th-axis milling operations for flat, contoured, and prismatic parts. CNC milling is often combined with turning and other operations as part of a full machining workflow.
Do you offer 3D printing services? ▾
Yes. Our additive manufacturing services achieve ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals. It's ideal for rapid prototyping, low-volume production, and geometries that are difficult to machine conventionally.
How do I get a quote? ▾
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, service needed, material, and timeline. Optionally attach a drawing or CAD file. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. The quote is always free and comes with no obligation.
How fast can you turn around a project? ▾
We work 24/7 to deliver the best service possible. Lead times vary by complexity, quantity, and material. Contact us with your specific project details and timeline requirements — we'll tell you honestly what's achievable.

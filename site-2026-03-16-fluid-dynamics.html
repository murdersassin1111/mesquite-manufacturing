Common Questions

We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on CNC machined and 3D printed parts. Our 4th axis CNC technology enables this precision even on complex geometries at high production volumes.
4th axis CNC adds a rotational axis (A-axis) to standard 3-axis machining. This enables machining of intricate geometries, undercuts, and complex curved surfaces in a single setup — reducing cost and improving accuracy for high-volume production runs.
We work with metals, plastics, and composites. Our 3D printing service extends this to traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metal additive manufacturing. Contact us to discuss your specific material requirements.

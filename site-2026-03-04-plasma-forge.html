Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything engineers and procurement teams ask before placing their first order.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We routinely achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across all our CNC machining and 3D printing services. Tighter tolerances are possible for specific applications — include your requirements in your RFQ.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth axis (4th axis) CNC machining adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis milling, enabling intricate geometries, undercuts, and complex contours in a single setup. This allows high-volume production of complex parts at significantly lower cost than multi-setup traditional machining.

What materials do you machine?

We work with a wide range of metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, brass, and more), plastics, and composites. Not sure what material your project needs? We'll help you choose in the quoting process.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Absolutely. Our Prototype Development service is designed for rapid iteration and design validation, while our Precision CNC Machining handles full production volumes with 4th axis efficiency at scale.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — precision CNC milling is a core capability. We offer both standard 3-axis and fourth axis CNC milling to handle flat features, complex profiles, and everything in between.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service produces parts to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — ideal for prototypes and end-use parts alike.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, attach a drawing or CAD file if you have one, and select your service. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week. Free quote, no obligation.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We operate 24/7 and work to accommodate tight timelines. Lead time depends on part complexity, volume, and material. Submit an RFQ and we'll provide a specific timeline for your project within 24 hours.

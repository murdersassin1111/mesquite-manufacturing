Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on our CNC machining and milling operations, and the same range on 3D printed parts — suitable for precision-fit components.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth axis (4th axis) CNC adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis machining. This enables intricate geometries, complex contours, and undercuts — all at high volume and lower cost than multi-step fixturing.

What materials do you machine?

We work with a wide range of materials including metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, brass, titanium), engineering plastics, and advanced composites — for both CNC machining and 3D printing.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes. We offer rapid prototype development for design validation, and scale seamlessly into production runs via 4th axis CNC machining — one partner from concept to volume.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — precision CNC milling is a core service. Our machines handle complex profiles, pockets, contours, and multi-face operations with high accuracy and repeatability.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. We provide additive manufacturing in plastics, composites, and metals — achieving ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances for functional engineering parts, not just concept models.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out our RFQ form below with your project details, material, and quantity. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week — no obligation, completely free.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We operate 24/7. Lead time depends on complexity and volume. Contact us with your project specs and we'll provide an accurate timeline — use the quote form to get started.

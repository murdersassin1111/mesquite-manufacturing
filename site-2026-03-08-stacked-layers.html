Common Questions

Engineering FAQ

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We routinely hold ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ tolerances across metals, plastics, and composite materials using our 4th Axis CNC equipment.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th Axis CNC enables rotation on an additional axis during machining, allowing intricate multi-face geometries to be cut in a single setup — at high volume and lower cost than our competitors.

What materials do you machine?

We machine metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, etc.), engineering plastics, and composite materials. Contact us to confirm your specific alloy or polymer.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Absolutely. Prototype Development is a core service alongside high-volume precision machining. We scale from a single prototype to full production runs.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — precision CNC milling is one of our primary services, delivered by our expert team with 4th Axis capability for complex geometries.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service achieves ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in plastics, composites, and metals — the same precision as our CNC machined parts.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out our RFQ form on this page. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week — no obligation, completely free.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Contact us with your project details and timeline — we'll give you a realistic lead time specific to your job.

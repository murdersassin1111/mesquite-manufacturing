Frequently Asked Questions

// Technical answers for engineers

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on CNC machined and 3D printed parts. Our 4th Axis CNC technology maintains these tolerances even on complex geometries and high-volume runs.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth (4th) Axis CNC machining adds rotational movement (the A-axis) to standard 3-axis CNC. This enables intricate geometries, undercuts, and complex contours to be machined at high volume and lower cost than competitors using conventional methods.

What materials do you machine?

We machine a broad range of materials including metals (aluminum, steel, stainless, brass, titanium, and more), plastics (Delrin, UHMW, PEEK, nylon, and more), and composites. Contact us with your specific material requirements.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes. We offer both Prototype Development (rapid, iterative, cost-effective) and production-volume Precision CNC Machining. Many clients prototype with us and scale directly to production using the same setup — no re-quoting or re-tooling required.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes — precision CNC milling is a core service. We provide full CNC milling across metals, plastics, and composites with 4th Axis capability for complex features and high-volume efficiency.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our 3D printing and additive manufacturing service delivers tolerances of ±0.001″–0.005″ across traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metal materials. Ideal for prototypes, complex internal geometries, or short-run parts.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — name, company, service needed, material, and project description. Attach a drawing or CAD file if available. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week, with a tailored quote at no obligation.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead time depends on project complexity, material availability, and volume. Submit your RFQ and our team will provide a specific timeline with your custom quote.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything engineers and buyers ask before submitting their first RFQ.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We hold tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across both CNC machining and 3D printing operations. 4th axis CNC allows us to achieve these tolerances even on intricate, multi-surface geometries.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth axis (4th axis) CNC machining adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis CNC, enabling intricate designs and complex geometries to be produced at high volume — at lower cost than competitors using only 3-axis setups.

What materials do you machine?

We machine metals, plastics, and composites. Whether you need aluminum, steel, titanium, engineering plastics, or composite laminates — we have the tooling and experience to handle it.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — prototype development and full production are both offered. We commonly help customers validate a design with rapid prototyping, then scale to production using the same setup and quality controls.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Yes. Precision CNC milling is a core service. We offer both standard and 4th axis CNC milling for parts requiring complex contours, pockets, and surface finishes.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service covers traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — all held to ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances. Ideal for complex geometries and rapid iteration.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, upload a drawing or CAD file if you have one, and we'll respond within 24 hours. We're available 7 days a week with no obligation.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead time depends on your specific project's complexity and quantity — contact us through the RFQ form and we'll give you a concrete timeline with your quote.

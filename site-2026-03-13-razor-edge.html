Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers

Everything engineers and procurement teams ask before their first order. Can't find your answer? Just ask us directly.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on CNC machining and milling, and the same tight range on 3D printing. For critical applications, discuss your requirements when you submit your RFQ.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

4th axis CNC adds rotational movement to standard 3-axis machining, enabling intricate geometries on multiple faces in a single setup. This allows us to manufacture high-volume complex parts at lower cost than competitors using only 3-axis equipment.

What materials do you machine?

We work with metals, plastics, and composites. Our 3D printing service also covers traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals. Specify your material when you submit your RFQ for a tailored quote.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes. We offer both prototype development (rapid, iterative, cost-effective) and full production CNC machining and milling. Many customers start with a prototype and transition directly to production — we handle both seamlessly.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Absolutely. Precision CNC milling is a core service. Combined with our 4th axis capability, we deliver complex milled components with tight tolerances across metals, plastics, and composites.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes — additive manufacturing with ±0.001″ to 0.005″ tolerances across plastics, innovative composites, and metals. Our 3D printing complements CNC machining to give you the right process for every part geometry.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the RFQ form below — describe your part, upload a drawing if you have one, and we'll respond with a custom quote within 24 hours, 7 days a week. No obligation, completely free.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7 and work around the clock to supply the best service. Lead time depends on complexity, quantity, and material. Include your timeline in your RFQ and we'll confirm availability in our quote.

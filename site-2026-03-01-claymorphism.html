FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything engineers and procurement teams need to know before requesting a quote.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?
We achieve tolerances of ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ across our CNC machining and 3D printing services — precision engineered for demanding aerospace, medical, and industrial applications.
What is fourth axis CNC machining?
4th axis CNC adds a rotational axis (A-axis) to standard 3-axis machining. This enables intricate geometries and complex features in a single setup — driving down cost on high-volume runs compared to multi-operation standard CNC.
What materials do you machine?
We work with a wide range of metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, brass), plastics (ABS, nylon, PEEK, Delrin), and composites — across both CNC machining and 3D printing.
Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?
Absolutely. Our prototype development service is built for rapid validation and design iteration, while our precision machining capabilities scale efficiently to high-volume production runs using 4th axis CNC technology.
Do you offer CNC milling?
Yes — precision CNC milling is a core service at Mesquite Manufacturing. We provide high-quality CNC milling with tight tolerances on metals, plastics, and composites, including 4th axis capability.
Do you offer 3D printing services?
Yes. Our additive manufacturing service achieves ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances in traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metals — making 3D printing viable even for precision-critical applications.
How do I get a quote?
Fill out the RFQ form on this page — describe your part, attach a drawing if available, and select your service. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week, with a custom quote tailored to your project.
How fast can you turn around a project?
We work 24/7 to supply the best service possible. Lead times depend on your part complexity, quantity, and material. Contact us via the RFQ form for a timeline specific to your project.

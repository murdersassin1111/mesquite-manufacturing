FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from engineers and procurement teams. Don't see yours? Submit an RFQ and we'll respond within 24 hours.

What CNC machining tolerances can you achieve?

We achieve ±0.001″ to ±0.005″ on both CNC machined and 3D printed parts. Our 4th axis CNC technology enables these tight tolerances even on complex multi-face geometries.

What is fourth axis CNC machining?

Fourth axis CNC adds a rotational axis to standard 3-axis milling, enabling machining on multiple faces in a single setup. This produces intricate geometries at high volume with lower cost than multi-setup alternatives.

What materials do you machine?

We machine metals (aluminum, steel, titanium, and more), plastics, and composites. Our 3D printing service also works with traditional plastics, innovative composites, and metallic materials.

Can you handle both prototypes and production runs?

Yes — we specialize in both. Prototype Development is a core service for rapid design validation. Once approved, we scale to high-volume production runs with the same precision standards.

Do you offer CNC milling?

Absolutely. Precision CNC milling is a core capability at Mesquite Manufacturing. We offer standard 3-axis milling and advanced 4th axis milling for complex geometries.

Do you offer 3D printing?

Yes. Our additive manufacturing service delivers ±0.001″–0.005″ tolerances on plastics, composites, and metals — often complementing our CNC machining for complex designs.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out our RFQ form on this page — it takes 2 minutes. Include your part description, material, quantity, and tolerance requirements. We respond within 24 hours, 7 days a week.

How fast can you turn around a project?

We're available 24/7 and work to your timeline. Lead time depends on part complexity, material, and quantity. Contact us through the RFQ form for a lead time estimate specific to your project.
